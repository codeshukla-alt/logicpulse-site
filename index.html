Privacy Policy for LogicPulse

Effective Date: March 3, 2026

LogicPulse ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application and services at logicpulse.live.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. This includes:

  • Account Information: We collect your email address and password via Firebase Authentication to secure your account. You may also sign in with Google.
  • Images & Text: When you take a photo or type a homework question, this data is processed to provide AI-powered solutions.
  • Audio: If you use the voice input feature, audio is recorded temporarily and transcribed to text for processing. Audio is not stored after transcription.
  • Usage Data: We collect technical data such as device model, OS version, and general location (country level) for app stability and localized curriculum support.
Optional Personal Information:

Users may optionally provide their display name, phone number, date of birth, gender, school, and grade level. This information is 100% optional and is used solely to personalize the learning experience (e.g., adjusting quiz difficulty to your grade level). Users can view, edit, or delete this information at any time in the app's Profile section.

2. Third-Party Services & AI Processing

Our app integrates trusted third-party services to function:

  • Google Firebase: Used for secure authentication, Firestore database, and cloud functions. Data is stored according to Google's enterprise-grade security standards.
  • Google Gemini API: When you use AI features (solving problems, quizzes, flashcards, code analysis, etc.), your prompts are processed by Google's Gemini models. Your data is used ONLY to generate answers and is NOT used to train public AI models.
  • Google AdMob: We use AdMob to serve rewarded video advertisements to free-tier users. AdMob may use device identifiers to show relevant ads. We have configured non-personalized ads only for enhanced privacy.
  • RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app subscriptions securely. RevenueCat processes purchase data through the Google Play Store.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide AI-powered homework solutions, quizzes, flashcards, and professional coding tools.
  • To store your solution history so you can review past work (you can disable this in Settings).
  • To manage your account, subscriptions, and preferences.
  • To improve app stability and performance.
  • We do not sell your photos, homework data, or personal information to advertisers or any third parties.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Images: Processed in memory only and not stored on our servers after the solution is generated.
  • Solution History: Stored in your Firebase account. You control it — delete individual items or clear all history anytime.
  • Account Data: Retained only as long as your account is active.

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data. Use the "Delete Account" feature in Settings, or email us at aditya@logicpulse.live. Data will be purged from our active systems within 30 days.

5. Your Rights

  • Access: View all your data in the Profile and History sections of the app.
  • Delete: Delete your account and ALL associated data from Settings > Delete Account.
  • Export: You can copy or share any solution.
  • Opt-out: Disable solution history saving in Settings > Preferences.

6. Children's Privacy

LogicPulse is designed for students. If you are under 13 (US) or 16 (EU/other regions), please use this app only with parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiers from children without parental consent. If we discover such data has been collected without consent, it will be deleted immediately.

7. Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Firebase Authentication secures your account with industry-standard methods.
  • Firestore Security Rules ensure only you can access your data.

While we strive for maximum security, no digital transmission is entirely immune to risks.

8. Permissions

The app requests the following device permissions:

  • Camera: To scan homework problems and code from photos.
  • Photo Library: To select images from your gallery for scanning.
  • Microphone: To use voice input for dictating problems.
  • Internet: Required for AI processing and cloud features.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Effective Date" at the top. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
